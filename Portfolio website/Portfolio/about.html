About Me

My journey into the video game industry began through my hobbies. Before the COVID-19 pandemic, I started collaborating with Zach Spangler, better known as YouTube's LoverFella. Working with his YouTube channel and Minecraft server ignited my passion for game development and design. This initial experience led me to join Vyce Games, a video game start-up, where I further honed my skills and deepened my interest in the industry.

At Vyce Games, I became more involved in the entire creative process—creating, designing, and developing video games. These experiences inspired me to expand my technical knowledge, which led me to enroll in Trinity College's Master program in Interactive Digital Media.
