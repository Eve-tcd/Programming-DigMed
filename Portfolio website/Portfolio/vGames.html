Vyce Games

Vyce games is an early stage start up organisation focused on harnessing the full potential of emerging technologies like Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, and Artificial Intelligence.

I began my work with Vyce Games in November of 2023. I was brought into the company as the Cheif Marketing Office but quickly rose through the ranks and being promoted to Cheif Of Operations by May of 2024. While at Vyce Games I have been heavily involved in the overall design and creation of our upcoming gamemode CIVS. We will be releasing CIVS through out featured partnership with Mineplex in December of 2024.

While I have heavily been involved in the creation and development of our gamemode, I have also been the main designer and editor of our website VyceGames. Here I have not only been the sole writer of all of our blog posts, but I also created and maintained our design to fit within our companies aesthetic.
